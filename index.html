About Me

Kissed & Lifted was created for girls who embrace softness while moving with confidence. I’ve always believed beauty should feel effortless — something that enhances who you are instead of covering it up. What started as a love for lip care and soft glam slowly turned into a brand built around confidence, femininity, and the power of feeling good in your own skin.

The Philosophy

At Kissed & Lifted, softness isn’t weakness — it’s power. Beauty should feel nourishing, intentional, and a little luxurious. Every formula is created to leave lips feeling hydrated, smooth, and elevated while embracing the effortless beauty of soft glam.

The Ritual FAQ

Is the formula vegan and cruelty-free?

Absolutely. Kissed & Lifted is 100% vegan. We never test on animals, and we don't use any animal-derived ingredients.

Are your products safe for sensitive lips?

Our formulas are made with gentle oils, butters, and skin-loving ingredients. If you have extremely sensitive skin, patch testing is recommended.

Do your products brighten dark lips?

Our overnight lip mask and routine help support smoother, softer lips and may help improve the appearance of uneven tone over time with consistent use.

How long does shipping take?

Once your order is processed, shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. You will receive a tracking number as soon as your ritual is on the way.
